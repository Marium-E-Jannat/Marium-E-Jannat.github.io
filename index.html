About Research Publications Teaching Contact

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science at the University of British Columbia – Okanagan, supervised by Dr. Mohammad Khalad Hasan. My research lies at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and ubiquitous computing, exploring interaction techniques for mobile, wearable, and AR/VR interfaces.

My work has been published at premier venues such as CHI and ISMAR. I’ve also collaborated with researchers across domains on interdisciplinary projects involving technology, behavior, and accessibility.
